Zenith Launcher

Minecraft: Java Edition for iOS & iPadOS

A powerful, flexible launcher that brings the full Minecraft Java experience to your Apple devices.

Get Started →

Features

🎮

Full Minecraft Support

Play any version from early beta to latest snapshots with full mod support.

⚡

JIT Compilation

Optimized performance with Just-In-Time compilation support.

🎨

Mod Support

Forge, Fabric, OptiFine & Quilt mod loaders fully supported.

🎮

Controller Support

Full keyboard, mouse & game controller support for the best experience.

📱

Jailbreak Optional

Works on both jailbroken and non-jailbroken devices via TrollStore.

🔧

Custom Controls

Fully customizable on-screen controls tailored to your play style.

Requirements

📱 Minimum

  • iPhone 6s or later
  • iPad (5th gen) or later
  • iOS 14.0+

⭐ Recommended

  • iPhone XS or later
  • iPad Air (4th gen) or later
  • iPad Pro (any model)

Quick Install

🎯 TrollStore (Recommended)

Best performance with automatic JIT. No computer required after initial setup.
